Featured Businesses

Boiler Business features the small businesses that have been the most popular for the week. These businesses have sold the most products, provided the most services, and have had the best reviews from their customers. Every Monday, the featured businesses will update using the most recent statistics to keep you up-to-date on what is popular in Boiler Businesses. In order for a small business to become featured, they must gain popularity among Purdue students and have positive reviews.
